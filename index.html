Who Am I?

Hey there! I’m Stuti—a Business Analytics and AI grad student at UT Dallas, but honestly, I see myself as more than just a numbers person. I get genuinely thrilled when I find patterns in messy datasets or when a dashboard finally brings complex information to life. But for me, analytics has always been about more than just crunching numbers; it’s about uncovering the real stories behind the data.

I love learning new things and diving into fresh experiences, whether that means mastering new tech tools or stepping up to organize events. What truly excites me is hearing about people’s journeys and what fuels their passions. Every person I meet has a unique story, and I deeply enjoy connecting, exchanging ideas, and sometimes simply listening.

Outside of building models or analyzing data trends, you’ll often find me trying things outside my comfort zone, volunteering, or participating in university activities. Curiosity is a fundamental part of who I am—it keeps me exploring, growing, and always seeking those moments when numbers meet human stories. So if you’re searching for someone as interested in people as she is in analytics, and who never says no to a good conversation or collaboration, let’s get in touch!
